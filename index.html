About Me

A Holistic Approach To Your Health

I'm a graduate of the FASTO Faculty (Faculty of Sport and Physical Education) and have spent over a decade coaching everyday people toward real, lasting results. Real transformation isn't about extreme physiques — it's about feeling good in your own body, having energy, sleeping well, and building habits that last.

My coaching goes beyond workouts. I help clients optimize blood markers, hormones, sleep quality, digestion, mood, and mindset. Whether you want to drop weight, build strength, fix your energy levels, or simply feel at home in your body — every program is built around your real life.
